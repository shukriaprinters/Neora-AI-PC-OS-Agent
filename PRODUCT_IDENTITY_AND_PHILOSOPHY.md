# NEORA DESIGN OS V2 - VOLUME 1, CHAPTER 1, PART 2: PRODUCT IDENTITY & CORE PHILOSOPHY
## Enterprise Master Specification (Version 3.0)

This master specification defines the permanent identity, creative philosophy, behavioral rules, and foundational values of **Neora Design OS**. These laws must guide every future development module, intelligent agent, plugin architecture, and system database design.

---

## 1. Product Identity

Neora Design OS is positioned uniquely in the creative software landscape. It is not an isolated point solution, but a fully integrated ecosystem.

```
       +-----------------------------------------------+
       |                 NEORA DESIGN OS               |
       +-----------------------------------------------+
       | Conversational AI | Professional Design Tools |
       |   (Intent Bus)    |     (Vector Canvas)       |
       +-------------------+---------------------------+
       |   Creative Logic  | Automation & Performance  |
       |  (Cognitive DAG)  |      (WebGPU Engine)      |
       +-----------------------------------------------+
```

*   **An AI Creative Partner:** Neora functions as a collaborative creative teammate that understands, plans, structures, and designs alongside the human operator.
*   **What Neora is NOT:**
    *   *Not a raw image generator:* We do not produce flat, uneditable pixels.
    *   *Not a static template library:* We do not force users into cookie-cutter layouts.
    *   *Not a simple chatbot:* Conversational logic is connected to an active layer-based vector canvas.

---

## 2. Product Personality & Core Philosophy

Neora's character is professional, composed, visually sophisticated, and deeply helpful. It is designed around the core principle: **The software must adapt to the user; the user must not adapt to the software.**

*   **Friction Reduction:** Every interaction must aim to reduce manual steps, unnecessary configuration options, and repetitive clicks.
*   **Aesthetic Calm:** The system values clean space, high-contrast typography, and a quiet dashboard layout over flashy or noisy elements.

---

## 3. The Design DNA

All design operations in Neora must trace through a strict cognitive pipeline. Design is never a random act of pixel generation; it is a calculated execution of brand rules and visual communication constraints:

1.  **Understanding:** Deep semantic parsing of user text, voice, or image reference.
2.  **Reasoning:** Cross-referencing audience intent, color psychology, and output constraints.
3.  **Planning:** Drawing up a strict grid layout, identifying layer groups, and scheduling resources.
4.  **Creation:** Instantiating vector paths, loading type styles, and rendering image reference emblems.
5.  **Review:** Auditing canvas coordinates for bounding box collisions, alignment errors, and WCAG accessibility contrast.
6.  **Improvement:** Fine-tuning layers and offering non-destructive styling suggestions.

---

## 4. Conversational Philosophy

Conversation is Neora’s primary bus. The system must support natural goals (e.g., *"Design an Eid banner,"* *"Create a luxury flyer"* or *"Make this typography pop"*) without requiring hyper-engineered, complex prompts.
*   **Intent over Exact Matches:** The AI must interpret semantic objectives, resolving localized contexts (e.g., translating traditional Bengali styles like "Pahela Baishakh") automatically.
*   **Interactive Design Control:** Prompting works bidirectionally. The operator talks to the canvas, and the canvas updates layers.

---

## 5. Autonomous Design Philosophy

When a design reference is supplied, Neora is designed to work autonomously, completing **80% to 90%** of the heavy visual preparation immediately.
*   **Concept Discovery:** Rather than offering a single choice, Neora proposes multiple conceptually distinct designs (e.g., Festive, Minimalist, Royal, Cyber) based on the image reference.
*   **Parameter Synthesis:** Layout, typography hierarchy, primary/accent colors, and vector framing are automatically composed without requiring the user to fill out endless setup forms.

---

## 6. Human Control Principles

While automation handles the repetitive tasks, **the human operator is the absolute decision-maker**.

*   **No Silent Replacements:** The AI must never overwrite lock states, change user-edited text layers, or modify color configurations without explicit approval or conversational instructions.
*   **Immutable Interactivity:** Every layout suggested by the AI must remain 100% interactive on the artboard. The user can override every single parameter manually at any stage.

---

## 7. Creative Reasoning & Visual Saliency

Before drawing any coordinate, Neora seeks to understand the "Why" of a design:
*   **Purpose & Industry:** Designing for a traditional printing shop in Dhaka requires different typographic weight and margin bleed than a digital corporate catalog.
*   **Saliency & Focal Points:** Key brand elements must be framed by geometric hierarchy. Accent details (such as vector lines or decorative borders) are programmatically anchored to keep layouts balanced and high-contrast.

---

## 8. Non-Destructive Editing Philosophy

Our workspace is built on non-destructive vector design paradigms.
*   **The Layer Paradigm:** Every text box, shape border, and reference image exists as an independent layer in the `layers` store.
*   **Dynamic Styling:** Properties like font size, border-radius, border-stroke, opacity, and blend modes are dynamic parameters, preserving complete editability for veteran designers comfortable with professional software like Illustrator or Photoshop.

---

## 9. AI Collaboration Philosophy

The AI does not dictate; it assists:
*   **Explainable Actions:** When the AI adjusts a layout, it outputs a clear, jargon-free log of *what* it adjusted and *why* (e.g., *"Aligned heading element to central safe area"*).
*   **Proactive Warning Systems:** The AI monitors boundary overlaps, low-contrast text layers, and canvas bleed-offs, suggesting subtle fixes without locking the screen or interrupting work.

---

## 10. Originality & Reference Extraction

*   **Respecting Copyrights:** Neora never duplicates copyrighted art.
*   **Structural Extraction:** When a user uploads a reference sample, Neora extracts only the core structural cues—such as dominant color palettes, relative layout groupings, typographic weight ratios, and stylistic moods—and recomposes them into entirely original, legally clean, editable art.

---

## 11. Knowledge & Asset Reusability

Every workspace session contributes to the system's local knowledge:
*   **Dynamic Templates:** Successful designs can be registered back into the workspace asset gallery as reusable style templates.
*   **Configurable Brand Books:** Color palettes, custom typography configurations, and vector borders can be saved and instantly applied to new image references in future design tasks.

---

## 12. Automation Boundaries

*   **Repetitive Production Automation:** High-frequency tasks—such as batch-resizing, border padding, language-translation, and grid-aligning—are fully automated.
*   **Irreversible Action Safeguards:** Destructive actions, such as clearing the entire artboard canvas or deleting historical save checkpoints, must require direct human approval or verification.

---

## 13. Absolute Accessibility Guidelines

Accessibility is baked into the architecture:
*   **Dynamic Contrast Checks:** The design engine calculates real-time contrast ratios between text characters and background layers, logging warning status below 4.5:1.
*   **Responsive Flowing:** Layout coordinates are percentage-based to allow fluid resizing across multiple display dimensions.

---

## 14. Multilingual & Script Preservation

*   **Equal Linguistic Support:** Traditional Bengali script, complex Arabic calligraphy ligatures, and Sanskrit styles are supported with high visual fidelity.
*   **Script-Aware Formatting:** Typography engines adjust tracking and line-height dynamically to prevent Bengali vowel signs (Kar) or Arabic diacritics from clipping.

---

## 15. Extensibility & Local-First Philosophy

*   **Decoupled SDK:** Future AI models, print adapters, and custom sidebar interfaces connect via a robust, non-intrusive hook architecture.
*   **Offline Operational Core:** Core editor functionalities—including canvas movement, layer ordering, and manual edits—must execute client-side, remaining fully functional even when network connectivity is lost.

---

## 16. Non-Negotiable Constitutional Rules

1.  **Never lock users into one model provider:** Core API calls must map through abstract adapter classes.
2.  **Editability is sacred:** Flat image render workflows are prohibited unless requested as final flat exports.
3.  **Explainability by design:** The system must clearly log its design reasoning in the user-visible system logs panel.
4.  **Absolute Creative Ownership:** All rights to generated vectors and layouts belong 100% to the user.
